Who We Are

Driven by quality, defined by delivery.

Our Legacy: Building Foundations for Tomorrow

ETHEL JOSHUWA PROJECT DEVELOPERS (PRIVATE) LIMITED is dedicated to being a foundational force in Zimbabwe's infrastructure and land development sector. The company's commitment involves turning undeveloped land into vibrant, fully serviced residential and commercial estates. This includes the full spectrum of civil engineering work, specifically the construction of complex road networks, reliable water and drainage systems, and the development of legally compliant, construction-ready stands. EJ Projects is willing to utilize superior materials, adhere to world-class engineering standards, and ensure meticulous project management to deliver high-quality, long-lasting infrastructure to its clients on time and within budget, underpinned by a core value of integrity and transparency.

Our work spans the full spectrum of civil engineering—from complex road networks and reliable water/drainage systems to the development of construction-ready stands. We pride ourselves on meticulous planning, adherence to strict quality standards, and efficient execution that ensures projects are completed on budget and on time.
